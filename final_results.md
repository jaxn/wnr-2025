## Legend

- **Position(Score)**: Finish position with points scored

## Scoring System

- **System**: Low Point
- **Novice Credit**: Points = max(1, finish_position - min(2, novice_count))
- **DNC/DSQ/DNF Penalty**: Varies by race
- **Throwouts**: 1 worst per 4 races (series rule)
